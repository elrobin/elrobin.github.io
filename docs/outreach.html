Academic service

In addition to research and teaching, I engage in applied academic work in collaboration with public institutions, funding agencies, and research organisations. This work focuses on research evaluation, bibliometrics, and science policy, and typically involves methodological design, analytical reports, and expert advice grounded in responsible and context-sensitive use of indicators.

Areas of expertise

  • Bibliometric and scientometric analyses
  • Design and assessment of research evaluation frameworks
  • Analysis of scientific collaboration, careers, and research trajectories
  • Methodological advice on data sources, indicators, and responsible metrics

Selected institutional collaborations

  • Bibliometric analyses and reports for Fulbright Spain or EC3Metrics among others
  • Research evaluation and assessment activities for public entities such as European Commission, the US National Science Foundation, the Swiss National Science Foundation or the Spanish Agencia Estatal de Investigación.
  • Advisory work for the International Centre for the Study of Research

If you are interested in collaboration or applied academic work related to research evaluation, bibliometrics, or science policy, feel free to get in touch.
